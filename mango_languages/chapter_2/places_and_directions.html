  • grande

    • big

  • Ese restaurante italiano es grande y la comida es buena, pero no está cerca de aquí.

    • That Italian restaurant is big and the food is good, but it's not near here.

Note

Grammar Note:

You use cerca to say that something is close as in Está cerca (it is close). When you want to express that a place is near another place, you put de after cerca as in cerca de aquí (near here).

  • Hay un cine a sies kilómetros de aquí.

    • There's a movie theater six kilometers from here.

Lesson 13

  • lleno de gente

    • crowed (full of people)

  • Sábado

    • Saturday

  • los sábados

    • on Saturdays

Note

Grammar Note:

To express that an activity is repeated several times or that it is always performed on a certain day of the week, we use th eplural form of the day. For instance, to say that a place is open on Saturday (meaning every Saturday), you say Está abierto los sábados.

  • Está lleno de gente lost sábados.

    • It is crowded on Saturdays.

  • Hay un cine a seis kilómetros de aquí pero está lleno de gente lost sábados.

    • There's a movie theater six kilometers from here but it is crowded on Saturdays.

  • Ese restaurante italiano es grande y la comida es buena, pero no está cerca de aquí.

    • That Italian restaurant is big and the food is good, but it's not near here.
