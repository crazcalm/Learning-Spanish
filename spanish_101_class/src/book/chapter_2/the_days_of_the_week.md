# The days of the week -- Los días de la semana

In order to express today's day we ask: ¿Qué día es hoy?
To respond, we say:

- How es lunes.
  - Today is Monday.

- Hoy es martes.
  - Today is Tuesday.

The days of the week are not capitalized in Spanish. In Spanish the first day of the week is lunes (Monday).

- martes,
- miércoles
- jueves
- viernes
- sábado
